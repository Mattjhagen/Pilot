Guiding Principles

1. Sequential Development: Phases should generally be completed in order.  Each phase establishes foundations used by subsequent phases.  Do not skip ahead unless a later phase is decoupled from earlier work.
2. Review & Approval: At the end of each phase, review the implementation against the success criteria described in the phase document.  Summarise what was completed and any decisions made in DECISIONS.md before starting the next phase.
3. Incremental Delivery: Even within a phase, prefer incremental commits and regular testing.  Avoid large, monolithic branches that are difficult to review.
4. Feedback & Adaptation: The roadmap is a living document.  Update statuses and adjust priorities as the project progresses or as new insights emerge.  Capture changes in DECISIONS.md.
